SABOR

Client: Sabor Restaurant


Target Audience:

Sabor is brick an mortar restaurant with capabilities that would include less expensive food truck dining options for events and pop up restaurant options made to order by customers for special events as opposed to catering. This dining experience will be Spanish culinary which is why the name Sabor was chosen, which means flavor or the passion behind the flavors. The target market of this restaurant is individuals between 26-42, college educated, income from $56,000 and up with an interest in food culture and interesting culinary experiences.


Description:

When branding Sabor we want the psychology behind color palette, menu fonts and item placement to give the customer an experience of a modern Spanish culture. The branding of Sabor will have an urban city art appearance, giving the customer an experience of Spanish culture through food and art.
